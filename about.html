Delicious Honey, Strong Hives About Our Apiary

Founded in 2021 in Columbus Indiana, Rob Bees Pure Honey is a small-scale apiary which has grown each year and provides fresh, raw honey and other products to our customers. After years of hearing about family members keeping bees and finding reasons why not to give beekeeping a try ourselves, we followed an urge, which has grown into a passion. We are dedicated to learning, teaching, and mentoring about this wonderful little insect, the honeybee, and the adventures in Beekeeping.

We have plans to continue to grow, add products, and services as we mature in this business. Perhaps even a dream of a store front at some point. Buzz back by to the Website, Facebook, and YouTube to see new and exciting stuff.
